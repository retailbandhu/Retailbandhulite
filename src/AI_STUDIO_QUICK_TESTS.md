## üí° **PRO TIPS**

### **Making Prompts Better:**
1. **Add examples** - Show what you want
2. **Be specific** - "In Hinglish" not "friendly"
3. **Set constraints** - "Under 50 words"
4. **Test edge cases** - Weird inputs
5. **Iterate** - Refine based on results

### **For Hinglish:**
- Don't force Hindi if English is clearer
- Use Hindi for common words (dena, chahiye)
- Keep product names in English
- Numbers in English
- Natural mixing is key

### **For Voice:**
- Test with typos (voice isn't perfect)
- Handle multiple interpretations
- Always include confidence score
- Provide clarification options
