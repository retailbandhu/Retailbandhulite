## Overview

**Retail Bandhu Lite** is India's first Voice + AI billing app designed specifically for kirana stores (small retail shops). The app enables shopkeepers to create bills by simply speaking in Hinglish (Hindi + English mix).

## User Types

### 1. Retailers (Kirana Store Owners)
- **Who**: Small shop owners, kirana store operators, local retailers
- **Goal**: Digitize their billing, inventory, and customer management
- **Entry Point**: retailbandhu.in â†’ /app
- **Authentication**: Mobile number + OTP

### 2. Platform Administrators
- **Who**: Retail Bandhu team members, support staff
- **Goal**: Manage platform, monitor stores, handle support
- **Entry Point**: /admin
- **Authentication**: Email + Password

## Summary

Retail Bandhu Lite provides a complete digital solution for kirana stores:

1. **Easy Onboarding**: Mobile login + quick store setup
2. **Voice Billing**: Speak in Hinglish to create bills
3. **Complete Management**: Inventory, customers, khata, expenses
4. **GST Compliant**: GSTIN, HSN codes, tax reports
5. **WhatsApp Ready**: Share bills and catalogs
6. **Offline Capable**: Works without internet
7. **Play Store Ready**: PWA can be packaged as Android app
